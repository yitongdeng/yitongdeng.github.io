Yitong Deng 邓宜桐

I am an incoming CS Ph.D. student at Stanford. My research interest lies in the intersection of computer graphics, computational physics, and machine learning. I strive to design numerical methods well motivated by physical and mathematical principles to faithfully and efficiently simulate the motion of real-world objects. Before joining Stanford, I received my M.S. from Dartmouth College, where I was very fortunate to work with Prof. Bo Zhu. I received my B.A. summa cum laude from Colby College.
