I am a second-year master's student at Dartmouth College, advised by Professor Bo Zhu. I received my Bachelor of Arts summa cum laude from Colby College with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Music, advised by Professor Bruce Maxwell. My research focus on computer graphics, around which I have explored topics in dynamic simulation, robotic control, and machine learning.
